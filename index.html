HOW

MASA is a sociocultural organization that aims to promote and spread the idea that Asians are not just a single group of people, but made up of many individuals from various distinct cultural backgrounds. We endeavor to share these cultural values to the UCSD community by providing a fun, welcoming, and stereotype-free social atmosphere for all who come to us.

As both a social and cultural organization, we focus on fostering an environment where you can not only make new friends, but celebrate Asian culture. From bonding over free food, to giving presentations about where specific Asian superstitions originate from, we do it all together. Together, we're not than just an organization--we're a family. We're a place you can call home away from home.

One of MASA's proudest traits is that we are all inclusive. There's no point system, no membership fee, and no requirements to join. One of our mottos is "You're already in"! Simply show up to our events, sit back, and enjoy your time with us--we'll take care of the rest! You can even make some new friends along the way. Check out our events here!

WHO ARE WE?

MASA, also known as Multi Asian Student Assocation, is one of the largest student-run organization exclusively at UCSD. Being an all inclusive org, there are no membership requirements; once you hear about us you're already in! We are the most welcoming and chillest organization you'll ever find. Join now!

FREE FOOD

Our Greatest Asset.

You read it right! Three words comes into mind when you hear the word MASA, Friends, Family, and FOOD. Yes, food! We understand the financial expenses and turmoil many students face when going to college. Our solution is to provide free food events for our members.
